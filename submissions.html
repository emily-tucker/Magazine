Spring SubmissionFest is Thursday, February 26, 2015

Stop by Quigley Hall rm. 129 from 1:30 – 3:00pm.

What is SubmissionFest?
Held once in the Fall, and once in the Spring, SubmissionFest is an opportunity for any student to enter their work for a $100 scholarship award. Students of all disciplines can submit their writing, or art (including but not limited to: short fiction, non-fiction, poetry, photography, or images of their original artwork). In addition to winning a potential scholarship, entries will be reviewed for publication in the Pathfinder Magazine and/or the online website.

Written Submissions
Emailed versions to be submitted as word attachements to pathfinder@western.edu . Typed, double-spaced, size 12 font, Times New Roman. Include student ID and contact information on a cover sheet.
Hard copy versions can be delivered to Quigley Hall during SubmissonFest hours.
Limit written submissions to four poems/prose and two short stories.

Artistic Submissions
Bring in your original artwork, or slides of your work, or a jump drive with your images on it. We will shoot/scan original artwork and return it immediately.
Limit artistic submissions to 3 pieces.
Use the form below to submit artistic files here. Online submissions may only be considered for website publication. File formats for web submissions should be 72dpi (dots per inch) and can be in the following formats: JPEG, TIFF, EPS, PDF. To be considered for scholarship and/or print publication you must submit files in person at SubmissionFest.
